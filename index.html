The Art + Technology Foundation is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to fostering global literacy in both technology and art. Our mission is to bridge the gap between these two dynamic fields, empowering individuals with the knowledge and tools needed to drive innovation and creativity. We strive to cultivate a deeper understanding of the intersection of technology and art, supporting the development of groundbreaking ideas, skills, and projects that will shape the future. Through education, collaboration, and resources, we aim to inspire and enable the next generation of creators, innovators, and leaders worldwide.
